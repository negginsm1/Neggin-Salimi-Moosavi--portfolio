EC2 instance would not launch on first try 
Problem: As part of a lab for an assignment, I had to launch an EC2 instance. Sadly, it would not launch on the first attempt. 
Investigation: Checked the settings of the EC2 instance such as the AMI and the instance type
Solution: Had to modify the instance type. 
What I learned: Always double check the settings before proeeding through the next step of an EC2 launch.
